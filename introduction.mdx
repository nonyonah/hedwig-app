Hedwig is a payments and business management platform built for African freelancers. Instead of juggling separate tools for invoicing, payments, client communication, and project tracking, Hedwig brings everything into one workspace — so you can focus on your work rather than chasing money or losing track of what's owed.

Whether you're a designer in Lagos, a developer in Accra, or a consultant anywhere on the continent, Hedwig gives you a professional way to get paid in USDC across multiple blockchains, create contracts and invoices in seconds, and stay on top of your client relationships.

## Key capabilities

### Get paid faster

Create professional invoices, proposals, and contracts in seconds with AI assistance. Share payment links that clients can pay with USDC — Hedwig handles the wallet infrastructure so you don't have to. Payments arrive directly into your embedded wallet on whichever chain your client prefers: Base, Solana, Celo, or Stacks (Bitcoin L2).

Pro plan users can also set up recurring invoices that auto-send on a schedule, useful for retainer clients.

### Track your earnings

Your dashboard shows real-time revenue, payment rates, pending invoices, and active clients at a glance. The Insights view gives you deeper breakdowns — conversion rates, payment history, and monthly summaries. Pro plan users also get tax summary reports ready for their accountant.

When your earnings are in crypto and you need local currency, Hedwig's integrated offramp supports conversion to NGN and GHS.

### Manage your workspace

Keep clients, projects, and milestones organized in one place. Attach contracts to projects, define deliverable milestones, and track what's been completed. The AI assistant surfaces the things that need attention — unpaid invoices, overdue follow-ups, upcoming deadlines — without you having to go looking.

### Email and calendar intelligence

Connect Gmail to automatically match incoming emails to your clients, projects, and invoices. Sync Google Calendar to see upcoming meetings alongside your project milestones. If a client emails you a contract or invoice, Hedwig recognizes and stores it automatically. You can also connect Slack to receive payment and invoice notifications directly in your workspace.
