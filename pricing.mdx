Hedwig is free to use for everyday invoicing and payments. The **Starter plan** ($5/month) adds recurring invoices, full revenue history, and daily brief emails. The **Pro plan** ($12/month) unlocks the AI assistant, OCR document import, milestone automation, and integrations. New paid plan signups start with a **7-day free trial** — no charge until the trial ends, cancel anytime.

You can review and change your plan from [hedwigbot.xyz/pricing](https://hedwigbot.xyz/pricing).

## Pricing and billing intervals

All plans are priced in US dollars regardless of where you live.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (save 20%) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | **$0** | **$0** |
| Starter | **$5 / month** | **$48 / year** ($4/mo) |
| Pro | **$12 / month** | **$115 / year** ($9.58/mo) |

The pricing page has a **Monthly / Annual** toggle at the top. Switch it to compare prices, then click **Start free trial** on your chosen plan.

<Tip>
  Annual billing saves you 20% and is the most economical option if you plan to use Hedwig long term.
</Tip>

## What's included

### Free plan

The Free plan covers the day-to-day essentials for freelancers and small teams.

- **Unlimited invoices**, payment links, and contracts
- **Creation Box** for natural-language invoice creation
- Crypto checkout on **Base, Arbitrum, Polygon, Optimism, Celo, and Solana**
- **1 external payout bank account** (Nigeria, Ghana, US, or UK)
- Mark invoices as paid off-platform with payment method and reference capture
- Clients, projects, expenses, and **last-30-days** revenue tracking
- Manual invoice reminders

### Starter plan

Starter adds automation and more headroom on top of Free.

- **Everything in Free**
- **Full revenue history** with no time limit
- **Recurring invoice automation** for retainer clients
- **Daily brief email** with top-line numbers
- **Up to 3 payout bank accounts**

### Pro plan

Pro adds the AI assistant, deeper automation, and integrations.

- **Everything in Starter**
- **Unlimited payout bank accounts** across multiple countries
- **Hedwig AI assistant**: chat with workspace context and smart suggestions
- **AI document import**: drag in invoices and contracts to OCR and auto-classify them
- **Automatic milestone invoice creation**
- **Gmail, Google Calendar, Drive, and Docs** integrations
- **Weekly summary email**

## Start your 7-day free trial

New Starter and Pro subscribers get a **7-day free trial**. You're not charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel from your account at any point during those seven days.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the pricing page">
    Sign in to Hedwig and visit [hedwigbot.xyz/pricing](https://hedwigbot.xyz/pricing), or open the **Account** menu and select **Upgrade**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Pick **Starter** ($5/month) or **Pro** ($12/month). Use the **Monthly / Annual** toggle to choose billing interval. Annual saves 20%.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Start free trial">
    You'll be redirected to a secure checkout page to enter your payment details. You're not charged today — your card is held for after the trial.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use features immediately">
    Plan features unlock as soon as checkout completes. The first charge happens 7 days later if you don't cancel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switch between plans or billing intervals

If you're already on a paid plan, you can change billing intervals or upgrade from Starter to Pro at any time from the pricing page.

1. Visit [hedwigbot.xyz/pricing](https://hedwigbot.xyz/pricing).
2. Toggle between **Monthly** and **Annual**.
3. Click **Switch to yearly billing** (or **Switch back to monthly billing**) below the plan card.

The change is prorated by your payment provider and takes effect immediately.

<Note>
  You can only manage subscriptions on the web if they were purchased through the web checkout. Subscriptions purchased through the iOS or Android app must be managed in the App Store or Google Play.
</Note>

## Manage or cancel your subscription

The **Manage subscription** link on the pricing page opens your billing portal, where you can update your card, download invoices, change plans, or cancel.

- **Subscribed on the web:** click **Manage subscription** to open the secure billing portal.
- **Subscribed in the iOS or Android app:** changes must be made in your **Apple ID** subscriptions or **Google Play** subscriptions.

Cancelling stops your renewal at the end of the current period. You keep paid access until the period ends, then move back to the Free plan automatically.

## When to upgrade

**Upgrade to Starter** when you need recurring invoices for retainer clients or want full revenue history beyond 30 days.

**Upgrade to Pro** when any of the following apply:

- You want the **AI assistant** to summarize your day, draft follow-ups, or surface unpaid invoices.
- You want to **drag in PDF invoices or contracts** and have Hedwig OCR and classify them automatically.
- You need **multiple payout bank accounts** across countries to receive offramped funds.
- You want **milestone invoice automation** for project-based work.
- You want **Gmail, Calendar, Drive, and Docs** integrations to work inside Hedwig.

If none of those apply yet, the Free plan covers the essentials and you can upgrade later without losing data.

## Frequently asked

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to use the Free plan?">
    No. The Free plan doesn't require a payment method. You only enter card details if you start a paid plan trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    Your card is charged for the interval you selected (monthly or annual) and your plan stays enabled. Cancel any time during the 7 days to avoid the charge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade from Pro to Starter or Free?">
    Yes. Cancel from **Manage subscription** to move to Free, or switch to Starter from the pricing page. You stay on Pro until the end of the current period, then move to the new plan. Your data, clients, and invoices remain intact.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade from Starter to Pro?">
    Yes. Visit the pricing page, select Pro, and the switch is prorated by your payment provider.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do plans sync between web and mobile?">
    Yes. Paid entitlements sync across the [hedwigbot.xyz](https://hedwigbot.xyz) dashboard and the iOS and Android apps as soon as your subscription is active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
    Web checkout accepts major credit and debit cards. Mobile subscriptions use the standard Apple ID or Google Play payment methods on your device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
