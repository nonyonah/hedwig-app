Hedwig is free to use for everyday invoicing and payments. The **Pro plan** unlocks the AI assistant, OCR document import, automation, deeper reporting, and unlimited usage. New Pro signups start with a **7-day free trial** — no charge until the trial ends, cancel anytime.

You can review and change your plan from [hedwigbot.xyz/pricing](https://hedwigbot.xyz/pricing).

## Pricing and billing intervals

Pro is priced the same regardless of where you live and is billed in US dollars.

| Interval | Price | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | **$5 / month** | Billed monthly, cancel anytime |
| Annual | **$48 / year** | Save 20% versus monthly, billed once per year |

The pricing page has a **Monthly / Annual** toggle at the top. Switch it to compare prices, then click **Start free trial** to begin.

<Tip>
  Annual billing works out to $4 per month and is the most economical option if you plan to use Hedwig long term.
</Tip>

## What's included

### Free plan

The Free plan is designed to cover the basics for freelancers who invoice occasionally.

- Up to **10 invoices** per month
- Up to **10 payment links** per month
- Up to **3 contracts** per month
- Crypto checkout on **Base, Solana, Celo, Polygon, and Arbitrum**
- **1 external payout bank account** (Nigeria, Ghana, US, or UK)
- Mark invoices as paid off-platform with payment method and reference capture
- Clients, projects, expenses, and **last-30-days** revenue tracking
- Manual invoice reminders

### Pro plan

Pro removes the monthly limits and adds the assistant and automation features.

- **Unlimited** invoices, payment links, and contracts
- **Unlimited payout bank accounts** across multiple countries, with a currency dropdown for clients
- **Hedwig AI assistant**: chat with workspace context, the Creation Box for natural-language invoice creation, and smart suggestions
- **AI document import**: drag in invoices and contracts to OCR and auto-classify them
- **Daily brief** and **weekly summary** emails
- **Recurring invoice automation** for retainer clients
- **Automatic milestone invoice creation** when project milestones are completed
- **Gmail, Google Calendar, Drive, and Docs** integrations
- **Full revenue history**, payment-source breakdown, and tax planning views
- Priority product updates

## Start your 7-day free trial

New Pro subscribers get a **7-day free trial**. You're not charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel from your account at any point during those seven days.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the pricing page">
    Sign in to Hedwig and visit [hedwigbot.xyz/pricing](https://hedwigbot.xyz/pricing), or open the **Account** menu and select **Upgrade to Pro**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a billing interval">
    Use the **Monthly / Annual** toggle to choose how you want to be billed. Annual saves 20%.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Start free trial">
    You'll be redirected to a secure checkout page to enter your payment details. You're not charged today — your card is held for after the trial.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Pro features immediately">
    Pro features unlock as soon as checkout completes. The first charge happens 7 days later if you don't cancel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switch between monthly and annual

If you're already on Pro, you can change billing intervals at any time from the pricing page.

1. Visit [hedwigbot.xyz/pricing](https://hedwigbot.xyz/pricing).
2. Toggle between **Monthly** and **Annual**.
3. Click **Switch to yearly billing** (or **Switch back to monthly billing**) below the plan card.

The change is prorated by your payment provider and takes effect immediately. You'll see a confirmation message once the switch completes.

<Note>
  You can only switch billing intervals on the web if your subscription was purchased through the web checkout. Subscriptions purchased through the iOS or Android app must be managed in the App Store or Google Play.
</Note>

## Manage or cancel your subscription

The **Manage subscription** link on the pricing page opens your billing portal, where you can update your card, download invoices, change plans, or cancel.

- **Subscribed on the web:** click **Manage subscription** to open the secure billing portal.
- **Subscribed in the iOS or Android app:** changes must be made in your **Apple ID** subscriptions or **Google Play** subscriptions. The web app will direct you there with a confirmation message.

Cancelling stops your renewal at the end of the current period. You keep Pro access until the period ends, then move back to the Free plan automatically.

## When to upgrade

Upgrade to Pro when any of the following apply:

- You send **more than 10 invoices, payment links, or 3 contracts in a month** and want to remove the cap.
- You want the **AI assistant** to summarize your day, draft follow-ups, or surface unpaid invoices.
- You want to **drag in PDF invoices or contracts** and have Hedwig OCR and classify them automatically.
- You have **retainer clients** and want recurring invoices to send themselves on a schedule.
- You need **multiple payout bank accounts** across countries to receive offramped funds.
- You want **full revenue history** and tax planning views for your accountant.

If none of those apply yet, the Free plan covers the essentials and you can upgrade later without losing data.

## Frequently asked

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to use the Free plan?">
    No. The Free plan doesn't require a payment method. You only enter card details if you start a Pro trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    Your card is charged for the interval you selected (monthly or annual) and Pro features stay enabled. Cancel any time during the 7 days to avoid the charge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade from Pro to Free?">
    Yes. Cancel from **Manage subscription**. You stay on Pro until the end of the current period, then move back to Free. Your data, clients, and invoices remain intact.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Pro sync between web and mobile?">
    Yes. Pro entitlements sync across the [hedwigbot.xyz](https://hedwigbot.xyz) dashboard and the iOS and Android apps as soon as your subscription is active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported for Pro?">
    Web checkout accepts major credit and debit cards. Mobile subscriptions use the standard Apple ID or Google Play payment methods on your device.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
