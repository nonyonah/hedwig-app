<Update label="May 2, 2026" tags={["web", "mobile"]}>

## New features

**Hedwig AI assistant.** A new assistant panel on the dashboard summarizes your day, surfaces unpaid invoices and upcoming deadlines, and gives you a one-sentence weekly insight. Switch between **Today**, **This Week**, and **Needs Attention** tabs to see what matters most. [Learn more](https://help.hedwigbot.xyz/introduction)

**Floating chat with attachments.** Drop bank statements, invoices, contracts, or receipts straight into the chat panel and let the assistant import them for you. You can attach up to six files in a single message. Bank statements automatically record both expenses and incoming payments.

**Composio integrations.** Connect Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, Google Docs, and QuickBooks through a single OAuth flow. Documents from your connected apps can be staged into Hedwig directly from chat.

**Multi-currency display.** Pick your preferred display currency from settings. Dashboards, insights, and payment views now convert and format amounts in your chosen currency, with live FX rates refreshed hourly.

**Project creation from a brief.** Send the assistant a project brief (text or attached document) and it'll stage a project with milestones, client, deadline, and budget — ready for you to review and confirm.

## Updates

**Smarter expense tracking.** Non-USD expenses are now converted to USD using live FX rates at import time, so your totals stay accurate no matter what currency the receipt was in.

**Revenue includes payment links.** Paid payment links now show up in the revenue summary, monthly trend, client and project breakdowns, and recent activity feed — alongside paid invoices. Your monthly target ring reflects the full picture.

**App download nudge.** New users get a friendly banner with TestFlight and Play Store links so they can grab the mobile app, plus a follow-up email with the same links.

**Clearer rate-limit messaging.** When you hit a rate limit (for example, on resending an email), Hedwig now explains what happened and how long to wait. Sign-in is faster too thanks to short-lived auth caching.

**Tax page removed from the sidebar.** Tax features now live inside the assistant and reports — visiting the old `/tax` URL redirects to your dashboard.

## Bug fixes

- **Milestone invoice emails** no longer show only a first name when the client's last name is missing — full names render correctly.
- **Calendar sync** has been removed from the mobile app while we rework the experience. The web calendar continues to work as before.
- **Expense category pills** render with the correct background colors and missing activity types now appear in the activity feed.
- **Slack integration** authentication and connection lifecycle have been hardened.

</Update>
